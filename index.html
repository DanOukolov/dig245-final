Systems of Control
The purpose of this Web App is to explain how the current economic system and tax codes have been engineered to work our human population and strip them of money for the benefit of the political and business elites.

The main proponent for my argument is an analysis of the shady founding of the federal reserve and the adoption of income taxes in the 16th amendment. I argue that if something is founded upon deceit and control, these economic tools and legislation are most likely still used as a means of control and corruption.

My arguments, historical and quantitative, are laid out across different pages on the navigation panel to the left.

A Different Perspective
 I hope to teach people to see hierarchy differently. In my opinion, hierarchy is most often earned by merit, but is always preserved by deceit.

I do not wish to convince people that a ruling class is playing around with our lives like puppet masters, but I do want people to see that there are clear winners and losers in our current societal structure. And it's clear that the winners want to keep winning by beating us losers as much as possible.
Rat Race Quiz
This quiz is designed to show users that they cannot simply work low paying jobs, pay taxes, and live a modest life if they want to retire with enough to last them until 80 years old. Late stage capitalism will require you to compete or die trying.
